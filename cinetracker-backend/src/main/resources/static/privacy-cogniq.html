Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 11, 2026

CogniQ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

Personal Information: We do not collect, request, or store any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address).

Game Progress: All game data, including completed levels, daily challenge completion status, streaks, and focus metrics, is stored locally on your mobile device. We do not sync this progress to any database or external server.

2. Third-Party Services

To support the development and operation of CogniQ, we integrate third-party services that may collect information sent by your device as part of their service delivery. These services include:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising): We display advertisements in our app using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use advertising identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID or iOS IDFA), IP addresses, device configuration details, and interaction metrics to serve relevant ads, perform ad analytics, and prevent fraud. For more details, see Google's Privacy & Terms.
  • Google Play Billing (In-App Purchases): If you purchase the premium ad-free upgrade, the transaction is handled securely by Google Play. We do not collect, store, or have access to your credit card, bank account, or payment credentials.

3. Data Deletion and Control

Since all your game progress is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it:

  • You can erase all your progress and history at any time by selecting the **"Reset All Progress"** button inside the app settings.
  • Uninstalling the app from your device will also delete all locally stored progress.

4. Children's Privacy

CogniQ does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children. Third-party advertising networks (like AdMob) may serve ads subject to target audience settings. We comply with Google Play's Families Policy and children's privacy regulations.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at your-email@example.com.
