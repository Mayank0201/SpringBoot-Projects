Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 18, 2026

CogniQ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

Personal Information: We do not collect, request, or store any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

Game Progress: All game data, including completed levels, daily challenge completion, streaks, achievements, settings, and focus metrics, is stored locally on your device. We do not store or sync this information to our own servers.

2. Third-Party Services

To support the operation of CogniQ, we use trusted third-party services that may automatically collect certain information as part of their services.

  • Google AdMob (Advertising): CogniQ displays advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID), IP address, device information, and interaction data to provide, personalize, measure advertisements, and prevent fraud. For more information, visit Google's Privacy Policy .
  • Google Play Billing (In-App Purchases): Optional purchases, including the ad-free upgrade, are securely processed by Google Play. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information such as credit card or bank details.

3. Data Deletion and Control

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information.

  • You can erase all game progress at any time using the "Reset All Progress" option in the app settings.
  • Uninstalling the app will also remove all locally stored game data.

4. Children's Privacy

CogniQ does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Advertising shown through Google AdMob is subject to Google's policies and configured audience settings. We strive to comply with applicable Google Play policies and children's privacy regulations.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date above will be revised accordingly.
