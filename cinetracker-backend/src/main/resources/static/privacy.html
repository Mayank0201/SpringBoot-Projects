Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 29, 2026

CineFolio ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we store your username and email address to manage your profile and synchronize your data.

App Data: We store your personal watchlist (Pending, Active, Completed movies) and the reviews you write within the app.

Community Interaction: We collect data on "Helpful" votes and XP earned to provide a gamified experience and community sorting.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our features:

  • TMDB (The Movie Database): We fetch movie titles, images, cast details, and trailers from TMDB. We do not share your personal data with TMDB.
  • Supabase / Render: Our backend infrastructure stores your account and app data securely.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Synchronize your watchlist across devices.
  • Display your reviews to the community.
  • Manage your XP rewards and profile status.
  • Improve app performance and discovery algorithms.

4. Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data (watchlist and reviews) at any time by contacting us directly at your-email@example.com. We will process your deletion request within 48 hours.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at your-email@example.com.
